Latest Character Nerfs

ASHE : Lowered the maximum damage and the shot recovery time to keep her overall damage output relatively the same. Aim-down-sights damage reduced from 80 to 75

HANZO : New rate of fire change, improved the feel and effectiveness of the Storm Arrows ability and though small, adds up over the course of firing 5 arrows.

SIGMA : Increased the cooldown of Sigma's barrier to require a higher commitment to its placement and opened up additional opportunities for counterplay.

WRECKING BALL : Reduced the amount of additional shields he generates per target though the base 100 shields from the ability remains unchanged.
